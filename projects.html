Dossier

Dossier gathers BIOS information and stores it in xml that is consumed by other tools to provide the information in multiple formats. Problems that are addressed:

  • BIOS information in memory is corrupted after boot.
  • BIOS information that is incorrect can be fixed for platforms with known problems.

RedX

This is an TCL interpreter with extensions for system debugging and control.
